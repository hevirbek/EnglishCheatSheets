There Is / There Are — Var / Yok

There is / There arevar
+
nounisim
Tekil → There is (There's): There's a doctor. · There's a pain in my back.
Çoğul → There are: There are many patients. · There are two tablets left.
Form Örnek (sağlık) Türkçe
There's a… There's a pharmacy near here. Yakında bir eczane var.
There are… There are many people in the waiting room. Bekleme odasında çok insan var.
There isn't… There isn't any medicine left. Hiç ilaç kalmadı.
There aren't… There aren't any free beds. Boş yatak yok.
Is there…? Is there a doctor here? Burada doktor var mı?
Are there…? Are there any side effects? Yan etki var mı?
Olumlu tekil
There is / There's + a doctor
There's a pain in my stomach.
Olumlu çoğul
There are + many patients

Türkçe Konuşanların Klasik Tuzakları

🚨 ache vs pain — baş ağrısı nasıl söylenir?
Türkçe "başım ağrıyor" → İngilizce'de genelde bileşik isim veya My head hurts. "I have pain in my head" doğru ama doğal değil.
✗ I have pain in head. · I have headache.
✓ I have a headache. · My head hurts. · I have a stomach ache.
🚨 have vs has — semptomlarda çekim
He/She/It ile has kullan — have değil!
✗ She have a fever. · He have a cough.
✓ She has a fever. · He has a cough. · I/You/We/They have.
much people — EN yaygın quantifier hatası
People sayılabilir (çoğul). Much sadece sayılamaz isimlerle.
✗ There are much people in the hospital.
✓ There are many people. · There's a lot of medicine.
There is + çoğul isim
Çoğul isim → There are (There is değil).
✗ There is many doctors here.
✓ There are many doctors here.
✓ some vs any kuralı
Olumlu → some · Olumsuz/soru → any
There are some pills. · There aren't any pills. · Are there any side effects?

Gerçek Hayat Diyaloğu — Doktorda

🔵 there is/are  ·  🟠 quantifiers  ·  🟢 should  ·  🔴 mustn't
Dr
Good morning. What's the problem?
Günaydın. Sorun nedir?
P
I have a headache and I feel dizzy. My throat hurts too.
Baş ağrım var ve başım dönüyor. Boğazım da ağrıyor.
Dr
I see. Is there any fever?
Anlıyorum. Ateş var mı?
P
Yes, I think so. There are a lot of people sick at my office this week.
Evet, sanırım. Bu hafta ofisimde çok kişi hasta.
Dr
It sounds like a cold. You should rest for a few days. Drink a lot of water.
Soğuk algınlığı gibi. Birkaç gün dinlenmelisin. Bol su iç.
P
Do I need medicine? Is there any pharmacy near the hospital?
İlaç gerekir mi? Hastanenin yakınında eczane var mı?
Dr
Yes. There's one across the street. Take some of these tablets. You mustn't drive after taking them.
Evet. Karşıda bir tane var. Şu tabletlerden al. Aldıktan sonra araba kullanamazsın.
P
Are there any side effects?
Yan etki var mı?
Dr
There isn't much risk, but you may feel a little tired. You don't have to come back if you feel better.
Çok risk yok ama biraz yorgun hissedebilirsin. İyileşirsen geri gelmek zorunda değilsin.
