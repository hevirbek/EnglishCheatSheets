📝 Mini Sınav

10 soru · ~15 dk

8+ doğru = Ünite 3'e geçmeye hazırsın. 6–7 doğru = zayıf konuları tekrar et. 5 ve altı = Ders 6–10'u baştan gözden geçir.

1. "Şu anda İngilizce çalışıyorum" — doğru cümle?
  1. I study English now.
  2. I am studying English now.
  3. I studying English now.
  4. I am study English now.
2. "Dün sinemaya gittik" — doğru cümle?
  1. We go to the cinema yesterday.
  2. We went to the cinema yesterday.
  3. We have gone to the cinema yesterday.
  4. We going to the cinema yesterday.
3. "Henüz ödevini bitirmedi" — doğru cümle?
  1. She hasn't finished her homework yet.
  2. She didn't finish her homework yet.
  3. She hasn't finished her homework already.
  4. She doesn't finish her homework yet.
4. Karlı hava görüyorsun ve "Kar yağacak" diyorsun. Hangisi?
  1. It will snow.
  2. It's going to snow.
  3. It snows.
  4. It is snowing tomorrow.
5. "Doktora gitmelisin" (tavsiye) — doğru cümle?
  1. You must to see a doctor.
  2. You should see a doctor.
  3. You should to see a doctor.
  4. You have see a doctor.
6. yesterday ile hangi zaman kullanılır?
  1. Present Continuous
  2. Simple Past
  3. Present Perfect
  4. Will / Going to
7. "Have you ___ been to London?" Boşluğa ne gelir?
  1. yet
  2. already
  3. ever
  4. just
8. "I ___ swim when I was five." (geçmiş yetenek)
  1. can
  2. could
  3. must
  4. should
9. "Okula gitmek zorundasın" (kural/dış zorunluluk) — hangisi?
  1. You should go to school.
  2. You have to go to school.
  3. You can go to school.
  4. You will go to school.
10. "Look! It ___ ." (şu an yağmur yağıyor)
  1. rains
  2. is raining
  3. rained
  4. has rained
