📝 Mini Sınav

10 soru · ~15 dk

8+ doğru = Ünite 5'e geçmeye hazırsın. 6–7 doğru = zayıf konuları tekrar et. 5 ve altı = Ders 16–20'yi baştan gözden geçir.

1. "Bu köprü 1990'da inşa edildi" — edilgen çatı?
  1. They built the bridge in 1990.
  2. The bridge was built in 1990.
  3. The bridge built in 1990.
  4. The bridge is building in 1990.
2. "Yağmur yağıyor, ancak dışarı çıktı" — hangi bağlaç?
  1. therefore
  2. moreover
  3. however
  4. because
3. "If it rains, I ___ stay home." (1. tip koşul)
  1. will
  2. would
  3. had
  4. stayed
4. She said: "I am tired." → Dolaylı anlatım?
  1. She said that she is tired.
  2. She said that she was tired.
  3. She told that she was tired.
  4. She said she am tired.
5. "Bu film diğerinden daha iyi" — hangisi?
  1. This film is gooder than the other.
  2. This film is better than the other.
  3. This film is the better than the other.
  4. This film is more good than the other.
6. "Unless you hurry, you ___ miss the bus." Boşluğa ne gelir?
  1. will
  2. would
  3. had
  4. can
7. "Bu ürün Türkiye'de yapılır" — edilgen?
  1. Turkey makes this product.
  2. This product is made in Turkey.
  3. This product was made by Turkey.
  4. This product makes in Turkey.
8. "He was sick. Therefore, he didn't come." — therefore ne anlama gelir?
  1. ancak
  2. bu nedenle
  3. ayrıca
  4. rağmen
9. "She is ___ student in the class." (en zeki)
  1. the most intelligent
  2. more intelligent
  3. intelligentest
  4. most intelligent
10. "He ___ me he was busy." (told vs said)
  1. said
  2. told
  3. asked
  4. spoke
