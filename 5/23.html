Türkçe tuzak: Bir paragraf = tek ana fikir.

✓ 1 konu + 3–4 destek cümlesi
Her paragrafın topic sentence'ı olmalı.

Paragraf Yapısı — Topic · Supporting · Concluding

Topic Sentenceana fikir
+
Supporting × 2–4kanıt / örnek
+
Concludingözet / sonuç
Altın kural: Bir paragraf = bir ana fikir. Yeni konu → yeni paragraf. Topic sentence genelde ilk cümledir; bazen son cümlede de olabilir (deductive vs inductive).
Bölüm Görevi Örnek ifadeler
Topic sentence Paragrafın ana fikrini söyler Learning English is essential today.
Supporting sentences Kanıt, örnek, açıklama, istatistik First… · For example… · Moreover…
Concluding sentence Ana fikri özetler / sonuç çıkarır In conclusion… · Therefore… · To sum up…
Standart paragraf akışı
Topic → Support 1 → Support 2 → Conclusion
Her destek cümlesi topic sentence'ı güçlendirmeli — alakasız detay ekleme.
📝

Essay Türleri — Kısa Genel Bakış

💭 Opinion Essay (Görüş)
I believe · In my view · It seems to me
"Do you agree that social media is harmful?" → Evet/hayır + 2–3 neden.
Soru: fikrin ne? → Paragraf 1: görüş + neden 1. Paragraf 2: neden 2.
⚖️ Advantage / Disadvantage
On the one hand · On the other hand · However
"What are the pros and cons of working from home?"
Paragraf 1: avantajlar. Paragraf 2: dezavantajlar. Son: dengeli sonuç.
🔧 Problem / Solution
The main problem is… · One solution would be… · As a result
"Traffic in big cities is getting worse. What can be done?"
Paragraf 1: sorunu tanımla. Paragraf 2: çözüm öner. Son: beklenen sonuç.
B1–B2 ipucu: Sınavda genelde 250–300 kelime istenir. 4–5 paragraf yeterli: giriş (topic + plan) → 2–3 gövde → sonuç. Her gövde paragrafı = tek ana fikir.
🔗

Linking Words — Yazmaya Odaklı (Ders 17 hatırlatma)

Konuşmada and / but / so yeterli olabilir; yazıda moreover, however, therefore gibi kelimeler paragrafı daha profesyonel gösterir. (Ders 17'deki 4 grup aynı — burada paragraf içi kullanım.)
Grup Yazıda kullan Paragraf örneği
Ekleme Moreover · Furthermore · In addition · Also …opens job opportunities. Moreover, it improves travel.
Karşıtlık However · Although · On the other hand · Despite Although it takes time, the results are worth it.
Sebep-sonuç Because · Therefore · As a result · Consequently Practice is essential; therefore, daily study matters.
Sıra First · Then · Next · Finally · In conclusion First, set a goal. Then, create a schedule.
Yazı tuzak: Although…, but… ✗ — although ve but aynı cümlede kullanılmaz!

Türkçe Konuşanların Yazma Tuzakları

🚨 Bir paragrafta birden fazla ana fikir
Türkçe'de uzun paragraflar normal; İngilizce akademik yazıda her paragraf tek konuya odaklanır.
✗ Paragraf: İngilizce önemli + hava durumu + teknoloji (3 konu)
✓ Paragraf 1: İngilizce neden önemli. Paragraf 2: Nasıl öğrenilir.
"I think" aşırı kullanımı
Her cümlede "I think" yazmak zayıf görünür. Alternatifler kullan ve kanıt sun.
✗ I think English is good. I think it helps jobs. I think…
✓ English is essential for international careers. Moreover, it provides access to global media.
Topic sentence eksikliği
Paragrafa doğrudan örnekle başlamak okuyucuyu şaşırtır. Önce ana fikri söyle.
✗ For example, I watch Netflix every day. (Ana fikir nerede?)
✓ Watching films with subtitles is an effective learning method. For example, I watch one episode daily.
Türkçe düşünme sırası → garip İngilizce
"Ben İngilizce öğreniyorum çünkü iş bulmak istiyorum" → kelime kelime çeviri yapma.
✗ I am learning English because I want to find work.
✓ I am learning English to improve my career prospects.
✓ "I think" alternatifleri — opinion essay için
Görüş bildirirken çeşitlilik kullan:
In my view · I believe · It seems to me · From my perspective · It could be argued that

Model Paragraf — "Learning English" (~100 kelime, yapı etiketli)

Mavi = topic sentence Yeşil = supporting sentences Mor = concluding sentence
Learning English is one of the most valuable skills a person can develop today. First, it opens doors to international jobs and higher education opportunities. Moreover, English allows you to access information on the internet, as most websites and research papers are written in English. Although mastering a language takes time and consistent practice, the results are worth the effort. Many Turkish learners find that watching films with subtitles and reading short articles help them improve faster. In conclusion, investing time in English learning pays off in both personal and professional life. Therefore, starting early and practising daily is essential.
Yapı analizi: Cümle 1 = topic (ana fikir: İngilizce değerli). Cümle 2–5 = supporting (iş/eğitim, internet, pratik, Türk öğrenci örneği). Cümle 6–7 = concluding (özet + tavsiye). Linking words: First, Moreover, Although, In conclusion, Therefore.
✍️

Pratik — Kendini Test Et

A) Outline oluştur — "Social media and teenagers"
Opinion essay için 4 maddelik outline yaz:
• Introduction (topic + opinion)
• Body 1
• Body 2
• Conclusion
Cevapları göster
Örnek outline:
Intro: Social media affects teenagers significantly; I believe the disadvantages outweigh the advantages.
Body 1: Advantage — connection with friends, access to information, creative expression.
Body 2: Disadvantage — addiction, mental health issues, reduced face-to-face communication.
Conclusion: Parents and schools should teach responsible use; overall, limits are necessary.
B) Informal → Formal yeniden yaz
1. I think English is super important for getting a good job.
2. Also, there's a lot of stuff on the internet in English.
3. But it's kinda hard to learn.
Cevapları göster
1. I believe English is essential for securing employment opportunities.
2. Furthermore, a significant amount of online content is available in English.
3. However, mastering the language can be challenging.
C) Topic sentence yaz — hangi paragraf?
Aşağıdaki destek cümleleri için uygun topic sentence yaz:
1. (Avantajlar: flexible hours, no commute, save money) →
2. (Çözümler: public transport, bike lanes, car-free zones) →
Cevapları göster
1. Working from home offers several significant advantages.
2. There are several effective solutions to reduce urban traffic congestion.
D) Hatayı bul ve düzelt
1. Although he is tired, but he continues working. →
2. I think English is important. I think it helps. I think travel is easy. →
3. For example, learning languages is useful. (topic sentence yok) →
4. I wanna get a lot of stuff from the internet. →
Cevapları göster
1. Although he is tired, he continues working. (but kaldır)
2. English is important for careers, travel, and communication. (tek topic + supporting)
3. Learning languages is highly useful. For example, it improves memory and career options.
4. I would like to obtain a significant amount of information from the internet.

5 Dakikada Özet

📐Paragraf = topic + supporting + concluding. Tek ana fikir.
📝Essay türleri: opinion · adv/dis · problem/solution.
🔗Yazıda moreover, however, therefore kullan (Ders 17).
👔Essay/e-posta = formal register. Kısaltma yok.
🇹🇷I think tekrarından kaçın; kanıt ve linking words kullan.
✍️Yazmadan önce outline yap — dinlemedeki ana fikir stratejisi gibi.
