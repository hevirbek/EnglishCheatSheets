Dolaylı Anlatım (Reported Speech)

B1 Gramer + Konuşma ⏱ ~2 saat
Türkçe tuzak: tell mutlaka nesne alır.
✗ He said me…
✓ He told me… / He said that…
Dolaylı anlatımda genelde zaman kayması (backshift) olur.
🔁 Ders 18 If clauses — koşul cümleleri First conditional — If + present, will + V Second conditional — If + past, would + V If I have time, I will call you.
🗣️

SAY vs TELL — Kural ve Örnekler

SAY TELL
Yapı
say (that) + cümle
that opsiyonel
tell + kişi + (that) + cümle
kişi zorunlu!
Olumlu
She said (that) she was tired.
Yorgun olduğunu söyledi.
She told me (that) she was tired.
Bana yorgun olduğunu söyledi.
Olumsuz
He didn't say anything.
He didn't tell me the truth.
Soru
What did she say?
What did she tell you?
YANLIŞ
✗ said me · ✗ said you
✓ told me · ✓ told her
Hızlı kural: say = söylemek (genel)  |  tell = birine söylemek (hedef kişi şart). Ayrıca: tell the truth / tell a story / tell a lie

Düz cümle (statement)
Özne + said / told + kişi + (that) + cümle
She said (that) she was busy. · He told me (that) he would come.
Direct → Reported örneği
"I'm tired," she said.
→ She said (that) she was tired.
"Yorgunum," dedi. → Yorgun olduğunu söyledi.
Reporting verb genelde geçmişte: said, told, asked, explained, warned… Cümle içindeki zaman bir adım geriye kayar (backshift).
⏪

Zaman Kayması (Tense Backshift)

Direct (doğrudan) Reported (dolaylı) Örnek
present simple → past simple "I work here." → He said he worked there.
present continuous → past continuous "I am studying." → She said she was studying.
present perfect → past perfect "I have finished." → He said he had finished.
past simple → past perfect "I saw her." → He said he had seen her.
will → would "I will help." → She said she would help.
can → could "I can swim." → He said he could swim.
may / might → might "It may rain." → She said it might rain.
must → had to "I must go." → He said he had to go.
Backshift yok mu? Durum hâlâ geçerliyse kayma olmayabilir: "The Earth is round." → Scientists say (that) the Earth is round. (hâlâ doğru)
🔄

you→I / we / he/she (bağlama göre)
this / these→that / those
here→there
now→then / at that moment
today→that day
yesterday→the day before / the previous day
tomorrow→the next day / the following day
last week→the week before
next week→the following week
ago→before / earlier
Örnek: "I'll see you tomorrow here."
→ She said she would see me the next day there.
❓

Dolaylı Sorular (Reported Questions)

Yes/No soruları
ask + if / whether + cümle
"Are you ready?" → He asked if I was ready.
"Do you like coffee?" → She asked whether I liked coffee.
Hazır mısın? → Hazır olup olmadığımı sordu.
Wh- soruları
ask + wh-word + özne + fiil
"Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived.
"What are you doing?" → She asked what I was doing.
Nerede yaşıyorsun? → Nerede yaşadığımı sordu.
Wh- soru — düz cümle sırası!
asked + where / what / when… + özne + fiil
✓ where she lived   ✗ where did she live   ✗ where she did live
3 kural: ① Soru işareti yok   ② do/does/did kullanılmaz   ③ Wh- soruda düz cümle kelime sırası
📢

Dolaylı Emir ve İstekler

Emir (command)
tell + kişi + to + fiil
"Close the door!" → He told me to close the door.
"Don't be late!" → She told him not to be late.
Kapıyı kapat! → Bana kapıyı kapatmamı söyledi.
Kibar istek (request)
ask + kişi + to + fiil
"Please help me." → She asked me to help her.
"Could you wait?" → He asked me to wait.
Lütfen bana yardım et. → Benden yardım etmemi istedi.
Olumsuz emir/istek
told / asked + kişi + not to + fiil
She told me not to worry. · He asked her not to tell anyone.
Özet: Emir → tell + to  |  İstek → ask + to  |  Olumsuz → not to + V
🇹🇷

Türkçe Konuşanların Klasik Tuzakları

🚨 He said me — EN KRİTİK TUZAK
Türkçe "bana dedi" kalıbından gelir. İngilizce'de say nesne almaz; tell alır.
✗ He said me that he was tired.
✓ He told me (that) he was tired. · He said (that) he was tired.
Backshift her zaman zorunlu değil
Durum hâlâ geçerliyse veya genel gerçekse zaman kayması yapılmayabilir.
✗ She said the sun was a star. (genel gerçek — gereksiz kayma)
✓ She said (that) the sun is a star. · She said she lives in Ankara. (hâlâ yaşıyor)
Dolaylı soruda soru kelime sırası
Türkçe'de soru sırası yok; İngilizce dolaylı soruda düz cümle sırası kullanılır — do/does/did gelmez.
✗ He asked where did I live. · He asked where do I live.
✓ He asked where I lived.
that unutmak / tell without object
tell mutlaka kişi ister. that opsiyonel ama cümle yapısını netleştirir.
✗ She told that she was leaving. · He told was tired.
✓ She told us (that) she was leaving. · He said (that) he was tired.
✓ Doğru karşılaştırma
Direct: "I will call you tomorrow." → Reported:
She said (that) she would call me the next day.

Diyalog Dönüşümü — Direct → Reported

🔵 Mavi = doğrudan konuşma  ·  🟢 Yeşil = dolaylı anlatım  ·  🟣 Mor = zaman kayması
A
"I'm going to the party tonight," said Anna.
"Bu gece partiye gidiyorum," dedi Anna.
B
"Are you coming with me?" she asked Tom.
"Benimle geliyor musun?" diye Tom'a sordu.
A
"I can't — I have to work," Tom replied.
"Gelemem — çalışmak zorundayım," diye cevap verdi Tom.
B
"Please call me tomorrow," Anna said.
"Lütfen yarın beni ara," dedi Anna.
Direct (orijinal)
Anna: "I'm going to the party tonight."
Anna: "Are you coming with me?"
Tom: "I can't — I have to work."
Anna: "Please call me tomorrow."
Reported (dönüştürülmüş)
Anna said (that) she was going to the party that night.
She asked Tom if he was coming with her.
Tom said (that) he couldn't — he had to work.
Anna asked Tom to call her the next day.

5 Dakikada Özet

🗣️say = genel söylemek. tell = birine söylemek (kişi şart).
⏪Backshift: present→past, past→past perfect, will→would, can→could.
🔄Zamir ve zaman: today→that day, tomorrow→the next day, here→there.
❓Soru: ask + if/whether veya ask + wh + düz sıra.
📢Emir/istek: tell/ask + kişi + to + fiil (not to = olumsuz).
🇹🇷Asla said me deme — told me veya said that.

Günün Görevi

6 Satırlık Diyalog — Dolaylı Anlatıma Çevir İki arkadaş arasında kısa bir konuşma yaz (6 satır). Sonra tüm konuşmayı reported speech ile anlat:
1. Bir düz cümle (said that…)
2. Bir yes/no sorusu (asked if…)
3. Bir wh- sorusu (asked where/when…)
4. Bir emir (told … to…)
5. Bir istek (asked … to…)
6. Zaman ifadesi içeren cümle (tomorrow → the next day)
Bonus: Backshift yapmadan da yazabileceğin bir cümle ekle ve nedenini açıkla.
