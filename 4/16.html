Pasif Yapı (The Passive Voice)

B1 Gramer + Haber Dili ⏱ ~2 saat
Türkçe tuzak: Pasif = mutlaka be + V3 (past participle).
✗ The door opened (aktif!)
✓ The door was opened (pasif)
✗ was open = açıktı (durum) ≠ was opened = açıldı (eylem)
🔁 Ders 15 phrasal verbs = fiil + edat separable · turn it off inseparable · look after break the ice · look forward to + -ing
⚡

Active vs Passive — Ne Zaman Kullanılır?

🔵 Active — Etkileyeni vurgula
Subject + verb + object
The chef cooked the meal.
Scientists discovered a new drug.
Şef yemeği pişirdi. · Bilim insanları yeni bir ilaç keşfetti.
Kim yaptı? önemli.
🟢 Passive — Eylemi / sonucu vurgula
Subject + be + past participle
The meal was cooked by the chef.
A new drug was discovered.
Yemek pişirildi. · Yeni bir ilaç keşfedildi.
Ne oldu? önemli; kim yaptı belirsiz.
📰 Haber / resmi dil
was built · is made · has been announced
The bridge was built in 1990.
Results will be published tomorrow.
Köprü 1990'da inşa edildi. · Sonuçlar yarın yayınlanacak.
Eylem ön planda; faili gizlemek veya bilinmemek normal.
🏥 Klinik / duyuru dili
Patients are asked to… · Smoking is not allowed
All visitors are required to register.
The test will be performed at 9 am.
Tüm ziyaretçilerin kayıt olması gerekmektedir.
Test saat 9'da uygulanacaktır.
Resmi, tarafsız, kurumsal ton.
Altın kural: Eylemi yapan kişi önemsiz, bilinmiyor veya açık ise → pasif tercih et. Türkçe'de "-ildi / -ilmiş" çoğu zaman pasife karşılık gelir ama İngilizce'de be fiili şart.

Passive: The bridge was built (by them).

Temel yapı
Subject + be (zamana göre) + past participle (V3)
English is spoken here. · The letter was sent yesterday.
Ajan ile (by + agent)
Subject + be + V3 + by + agent
The book was written by Orwell. · The cake was made by my mother.
Olumsuz
Subject + be + not + V3
Smoking is not allowed. · The window wasn't opened.
Soru
Be + subject + V3?
Is English spoken here? · Was the door opened? · Has the report been finished?
V3 nasıl bulunur? Düzenli fiiller: -ed (opened, cleaned). Düzensiz: 2. sütun veya 3. sütun (write → written, make → made, build → built). Pasifte asla yalın fiil kullanılmaz.

Pasif Zamanlar — Tense Table

Zaman Aktif örnek Pasif formül Pasif örnek Türkçe
Present Simple They make cars here. am/is/are + V3 Cars are made here. Arabalar burada üretilir.
Past Simple Someone stole my bike. was/were + V3 My bike was stolen. Bisikletim çalındı.
Future (will) They will announce the results. will be + V3 The results will be announced tomorrow. Sonuçlar yarın açıklanacak.
Present Perfect They have finished the report. have/has been + V3 The report has been finished. Rapor tamamlanmış / bitti.
Present Continuous They are repairing the road. am/is/are being + V3 The road is being repaired. Yol onarılıyor (şu an).
Past Continuous They were filming the scene. was/were being + V3 The scene was being filmed when we arrived. Sahne çekiliyordu.
Modal (can/must/should) You must finish this today. modal + be + V3 This must be finished today. Bu bugün bitirilmeli.
Present Perfect Passive: has/have been + V3 — yakın geçmişte tamamlanan eylem, sonuç şimdi geçerli.
A new hospital has been opened. = Yeni bir hastane açıldı (ve hâlâ açık).
Future Passive: will be + V3 veya is going to be + V3
The meeting will be held on Monday. = Toplantı Pazartesi yapılacak.

Türkçe Konuşanların Klasik Tuzakları

🚨 was opened vs was open — EN KRİTİK TUZAK
was opened = eylem (biri açtı / açıldı). was open = durum (açıktı, kapalı değildi). Türkçe "açıldı" ile "açıktı" karışır!
✓ The shop was opened at 9. = Mağaza saat 9'da açıldı. (eylem)
✓ The shop was open until 6. = Mağaza 6'ya kadar açıktı. (durum — adjective)
✗ The door was open by the wind. → The door was opened by the wind.
🚨 "be" fiilini unutmak — pasif değil, aktif kalır!
Türkçe'de "-ildi" yeter; İngilizce'de mutlaka be + V3 gerekir. Yalın fiil veya sadece V3 pasif değildir.
✗ The letter sent yesterday. · The window broken.
✓ The letter was sent yesterday. · The window was broken.
✗ English speak here. → English is spoken here.
by who? → by whom?
Resmi İngilizce'de "kim tarafından?" sorusu By whom? olur. Konuşmada Who … by? da kullanılır.
✗ By who was it written?
✓ By whom was it written? (formal) · Who was it written by? (informal)
V2 kullanmak (opened ✓ ama opened alone ✗)
Pasifte past participle (V3) gerekir. Düzensiz fiillerde V2 ≠ V3 olabilir.
✗ The book was wrote by her. · It was taked.
✓ The book was written by her. · It was taken.
Intransitive fiiller pasif olmaz
Nesne almayan fiiller (arrive, happen, sleep, die) pasife çevrilemez.
✗ The accident was happened yesterday.
✓ The accident happened yesterday. (aktif kalır)
✓ get + past participle (informal passive)
Konuşmada get pasif anlamı verir — özellikle beklenmedik / olumsuz olaylarda.
My phone got stolen. · He got fired. = was stolen / was fired (daha informal)
🔍

was opened vs was open — Hızlı Referans

İfade Tür Anlam Örnek
was/were + V3 Pasif (eylem) -ildi, -ilmiş The bank was robbed.
was/were + adjective Durum (sıfat) -di (durum) The bank was closed (kapalıydı).
was/were + V3 Pasif (eylem) kapatıldı The bank was closed by the manager.
is/are + V3 Genel pasif -ilir, -ilir Turkish is spoken here.
is/are + adjective Durum -dir (durum) The door is open.
İpucu: Cümlede by + agent varsa neredeyse kesin pasiftir: was closed by the manager. Agent yoksa bağlama bak: eylem mi, durum mu?
Sık karışan çiftler
was broken = kırıldı  |  was broken (injured) = yaralıydı
was interested = ilgilendi  |  was interesting = ilginçti
was surprised = şaşırdı  |  was surprising = şaşırtıcıydı

Gerçek Hayat — Hastane Duyurusu ve Haber Bülteni

Hospital announcement: Attention please. All patients are asked to remain in the waiting area. The blood test will be performed in Room 4. Mobile phones must be switched off in the examination rooms. Thank you for your cooperation.
Hastane duyurusu: Dikkat lütfen. Tüm hastalar bekleme alanında kalmaya davet edilmektedir. Kan testi 4 numaralı odada uygulanacaktır. Muayene odalarında cep telefonları kapatılmalıdır.
N
Good evening. A new health centre has been opened in the city centre. The building was designed by a local architect and was built in just eighteen months.
İyi akşamlar. Şehir merkezinde yeni bir sağlık merkezi açıldı. Bina yerel bir mimar tarafından tasarlandı ve sadece on sekiz ayda inşa edildi.
R
How many people will be served there?
Orada kaç kişiye hizmet verilecek?
N
About five thousand patients are expected to be treated every month. Free vaccinations will also be offered to children under twelve.
Her ay yaklaşık beş bin hastanın tedavi edilmesi bekleniyor. On iki yaş altı çocuklara ücretsiz aşılar da sunulacak.
R
When was the project first announced?
Proje ilk ne zaman duyuruldu?
N
It was announced by the mayor last year, but construction was delayed because of bad weather. The centre is now open to the public — and yes, that means it is open, not "opened"! The doors were officially opened at 10 am this morning.
Geçen yıl belediye başkanı tarafından duyuruldu, ancak kötü hava nedeniyle inşaat gecikti. Merkez artık halka açık — evet, "açık" demek istiyoruz, "açıldı" değil! Kapılar bu sabah saat 10'da resmen açıldı.

2. Someone stole my wallet. →

4. The chef cooked the meal. →
5. They have finished the project. →
Cevapları göster
1. This bridge was built in 2005.
2. My wallet was stolen.
3. The results will be announced tomorrow.
4. The meal was cooked (by the chef).
5. The project has been finished.
B) Doğru be + V3 formunu seç
1. English (speak) in many countries.
2. The window (break) last night.
3. The meeting (hold) next Monday.
4. My keys (lose) — I can't find them.
5. The road (repair) at the moment.
Cevapları göster
1. is spoken   2. was broken   3. will be held   4. have been lost   5. is being repaired
C) was opened mi, was open mı?
1. The shop at 8 am every day. (eylem — her gün açılıyor)
2. The shop until midnight yesterday. (durum — gece yarısına kadar açıktı)
3. The new library by the mayor last week. (eylem)
4. The door — please close it. (durum — şu an açık)
Cevapları göster
1. is opened (veya opens — aktif de olabilir)   2. was open
3. was opened   4. is open
D) Hatayı bul ve düzelt

2. The book was wrote by her. →

4. The accident was happened on Monday. →
