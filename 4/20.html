Karşılaştırma Kuralları — -er / more / -est / most

📏 Kısa sıfat (1–2 hece)
adj + -er · adj + -est
tall → taller → tallest
big → bigger → biggest (son sessiz çiftlenir)
hot → hotter → hottest
uzun → daha uzun → en uzun
📚 Uzun sıfat (2+ hece)
more + adj · most + adj
expensive → more expensive → most expensive
beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful
interesting → more interesting → most interesting
pahalı → daha pahalı → en pahalı
✏️ -y ile biten sıfatlar
y → i + -er / -est
happy → happier → happiest
easy → easier → easiest
busy → busier → busiest
mutlu → daha mutlu → en mutlu

Hece kuralı (genel): 1 hece (big, hot) veya 2 hece + tek sesli (happy, easy) → -er/-est. Uzun kelimeler (beautiful, expensive) → more/most. Emin değilsen more genelde güvenli.

Eşitlik — as…as / not as…as / the same as

✅ Aynı derece — as + adj + as
as … as = kadar …
She is as tall as her brother.
This book is as interesting as that one.
He runs as fast as a professional.
Kardeşi kadar uzun. · Bu kitap o kadar ilginç. · Profesyonel kadar hızlı koşuyor.
❌ Daha az — not as…as / not so…as
not as … as = … kadar … değil
This phone is not as expensive as that one.
I'm not as busy as I was last week.
It's not so cold as yesterday. (= aynı anlam)
Bu telefon o kadar pahalı değil. · Geçen haftaki kadar meşgul değilim.
🔄 Aynı şey — the same as
the same + noun + as
My bag is the same as yours.
We have the same phone as them.
Her salary is the same as mine.
Çantam seninkiyle aynı. · Onlarla aynı telefona sahibiz.
Eşitlik
Özne + as + adj + as + karşılaştırılan
Istanbul is as big as London. (London kadar büyük)
Eşit değil
Özne + not as + adj + as
Ankara is not as crowded as Istanbul.
📦

İsimlerle Karşılaştırma — more / fewer / less + noun

➕ Daha fazla — more + noun + than
more + isim (sayılabilen/sayılamayan)
I have more books than you.
She spends more money than her sister.
There are more people than yesterday.
Senden daha fazla kitabım var. · Kız kardeşinden daha fazla para harcıyor.
➖ Sayılabilen — fewer + noun + than
fewer + çoğul isim
This shop has fewer customers than last year.
I made fewer mistakes than before.
There are fewer cars than bikes here.
Bu mağazada geçen yıla göre daha az müşteri var.
➖ Sayılamayan — less + noun + than
less + tekil / sayılamayan isim
I have less time than you.
This model uses less energy than the old one.
She drinks less coffee than me.
Senden daha az zamanım var. · Bu model eskisinden daha az enerji kullanıyor.
Fewer vs Less: fewer = sayılabilen (fewer apples)  |  less = sayılamayan (less sugar). Günlük İngilizce'de "less people" duyulur ama sınavda fewer people doğru.

Türkçe Konuşanların Klasik Tuzakları

🚨 more better / more worse — ÇİFT KARŞILAŞTIRMA
Düzensiz sıfatlar zaten karşılaştırma formundadır; more eklenmez!
✗ This is more better than that.
✓ This is better than that.
✗ more worse · more best
✓ worse · the best
than vs from
Türkçe "-den" karşılaştırması İngilizce'de than ile yapılır, from değil.
✗ She is taller from me.
✓ She is taller than me. / than I am.
Double comparative — daha daha
Bir cümlede iki karşılaştırma formu kullanılmaz.
✗ more easier · more bigger · the most best
✓ easier · bigger · the best
Superlative'te the unutmak
Türkçe "en büyük şehir" derken İngilizce'de the şart.
✗ Istanbul is biggest city.
✓ Istanbul is the biggest city in Turkey.
✓ than me vs than I am
Günlük konuşmada "than me" normal. Resmi yazıda "than I am" tercih edilir — ikisi de kabul görür.
She is taller than me. ✓ · She is taller than I am. ✓ (formal)

Gerçek Hayat Diyaloğu — Alışverişte Ürün Karşılaştırma

🟢 Yeşil = comparative (-er / more)  ·  🟣 Mor = superlative (the -est / the most)  ·  🔵 Mavi = as…as / the same as
S
Hi! Can I help you compare these two laptops?
Merhaba! Bu iki laptopu karşılaştırmanıza yardım edebilir miyim?
C
Yes, please. Which one is lighter?
Evet lütfen. Hangisi daha hafif?
S
The silver one is lighter than the black one — only 1.2 kg. But the black one is more powerful.
Gümüş olan siyah olandan daha hafif — sadece 1,2 kg. Ama siyah olan daha güçlü.
C
Is the screen as big as on the other model?
Ekran diğer modeldeki kadar büyük mü?
S
Almost — it's not as large as the black one, but the battery life is better. And it's cheaper than all the others in the shop.
Neredeyse — siyah o kadar büyük değil, ama pil ömrü daha iyi. Ve mağazadaki diğerlerinden daha ucuz.
C
What about the phone? I need something with more storage than my old one.
Telefon ne olacak? Eskisinden daha fazla depolamaya ihtiyacım var.
S
This model has less memory than the Pro, but the camera is the best in this price range. It's the same as the one your friend bought last month.
Bu modelde Pro'dan daha az bellek var, ama kamera bu fiyat aralığındaki en iyisi. Arkadaşınızın geçen ay aldığıyla aynı.
C
Perfect! The silver laptop and this phone — are they the most popular products here?
Mükemmel! Gümüş laptop ve bu telefon — buradaki en popüler ürünler mi?
S
The phone is the most popular, yes. The laptop is more popular than the gaming models but not as popular as our budget tablets.
Telefon en popüler, evet. Laptop oyun modellerinden daha popüler ama bütçe tabletlerimiz kadar popüler değil.

Pratik — Kendini Test Et

A) Comparative veya superlative yaz
1. This hotel is (good) than the last one.
2. She is (the / tall) student in the class.
3. Today is (hot) than yesterday.
4. It was (bad) exam I've ever taken.
5. This route is (far) — let's take it.
Cevapları göster
1. better   2. the tallest   3. hotter   4. the worst   5. farther / further
B) as…as / not as…as / the same as
1. My room is yours. (aynı büyüklükte)
2. This coffee is the one in Italy. (o kadar iyi değil)
3. His car is mine. (aynı model)
4. She doesn't earn her colleague. (aynı miktarda değil)
Cevapları göster
1. as big as   2. not as good as   3. the same as   4. as much as
C) Hatayı bul ve düzelt
1. She is more taller than me. →
2. This is the most best restaurant. →
3. Istanbul is bigger from Ankara. →
4. I have less friends than you. →
Cevapları göster
1. She is taller than me.   2. This is the best restaurant.   3. Istanbul is bigger than Ankara.   4. I have fewer friends than you.
D) Cümle tamamla — isim karşılaştırma
1. There are (few) students in this class than last year.
2. I need (much) time to finish.
3. She bought (many) books than her brother.
4. This phone has (little) battery life than the Pro model.
Cevapları göster
1. fewer   2. more   3. more   4. less

5 Dakikada Özet

📏Kısa sıfat: -er / -est. Uzun: more / most. -y → -ier/-iest.
⚡Düzensiz: good→better→best, bad→worse→worst, far→further, little→less→least.
⚖️Eşitlik: as + adj + as. Olumsuz: not as…as. Aynı: the same as.
📦İsim: more/fewer/less + noun + than.
🏆Superlative: the + -est / the most (+ in/of grup).
🇹🇷Asla more better. Karşılaştırma = than, from değil.

Günün Görevi — Ünite 4 Capstone
