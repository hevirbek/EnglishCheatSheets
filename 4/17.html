Cümle Bağlama (Linking Words)

B1 Yazma + Konuşma ⏱ ~2 saat
Türkçe tuzak: Bağlaçlar cümleleri birbirine mantıksal olarak bağlar.
✗ Although it rained, but we went out.
✓ Although it rained, we went out.
Although ve but aynı cümlede kullanılmaz!
🔁 Geçen ders Pasif yapı = be + past participle The report was written by the team. The meeting is held every Monday. Odak: eylem / nesne (özne değil)

4 ana grup: ① Ekleme (and, moreover)   ② Karşıtlık (but, however)   ③ Sebep-sonuç (because, therefore)   ④ Sıra/zaman (first, then, finally)
İki bağımsız cümle (virgül + linker)
Cümle 1 , and / but / so cümle 2
I was tired, so I went home early.
Bağımlı cümle (although / because / since)
Although / Because clause , main clause
Although it was late, we continued working.
Despite / in spite of (+ isim veya -ing)
Despite the rain / being tired , main clause
Despite the rain, we went out. · Despite being tired, she stayed.
🗺️

4 Kategori — Hızlı Harita

➕ Ekleme (addition)
and · also · moreover · in addition · furthermore
I speak English. Also, I speak German.
İngilizce konuşurum. Ayrıca Almanca da konuşurum.
↔️ Karşıtlık (contrast)
but · however · although · though · despite / in spite of
It was expensive. However, the quality was excellent.
Pahalıydı. Ancak kalite mükemmeldi.
⚡ Sebep & Sonuç (cause & effect)
because · since · so · therefore · as a result · consequently
The flight was delayed because of bad weather.
Uçuş kötü hava yüzünden gecikti.
🔢 Sıra & Zaman (sequence / time)
first · then · next · finally · meanwhile · after that
First, we checked in. Then, we went to the gate.
Önce check-in yaptık. Sonra kapıya gittik.
Yeşil = ekleme Kırmızı = karşıtlık Turuncu = sebep-sonuç Mavi = sıra/zaman
➕

1. Ekleme — Addition

Bağlaç Kullanım Örnek
and Basit ekleme · iki eşit fikir She works hard and she learns fast.
also Cümle başı/ortası · "ayrıca" I also need a visa. · Also, bring your passport.
moreover Resmi yazı · güçlü ekleme The hotel is cheap. Moreover, it's near the beach.
in addition Resmi · "buna ek olarak" In addition, the company offers free training.
furthermore Akademik / iş yazısı Sales rose 10%. Furthermore, costs fell.
Resmiyet sırası: and < also < in addition < moreover / furthermore. İş e-postasında moreover kullanmak daha profesyonel görünür.
↔️

2. Karşıtlık — Contrast

Bağlaç Yapı Örnek
but Cümle ortası · virgülden sonra I wanted to go, but I was too tired.
however Cümle başı · virgül · resmi The job is stressful. However, the salary is good.
although + tam cümle (özne + fiil) Although he is young, he is very experienced.
though although ile aynı · daha konuşma dili Though it was raining, we played football.
despite / in spite of + isim / -ing (fiil değil!) Despite the traffic, we arrived on time.
Although vs Despite: Although it rained (cümle)  |  Despite the rain (isim). İkisi de "rağmen" anlamı verir — yapı farklı!
⚡

3. Sebep & Sonuç — Cause & Effect

Bağlaç Rol Örnek
because Sebep (en yaygın) We cancelled the trip because the weather was bad.
since Sebep · "madem / -dığı için" Since you're here, let's start the meeting.
so Sonuç · konuşma dili I missed the bus, so I was late for work.
therefore Sonuç · resmi yazı The data was incomplete. Therefore, we postponed the report.
as a result Sonuç ifadesi He didn't study. As a result, he failed the exam.
consequently Sonuç · akademik Costs increased. Consequently, prices went up.
Because vs So: because = sebep önce gelir  |  so = sonuç önce gelir.
✓ I stayed home because I was sick.  ·  ✓ I was sick, so I stayed home.
🔢

4. Sıra & Zaman — Sequence / Time

Bağlaç Anlam Örnek
first İlk adım First, open the app and log in.
then Sonra · ardından Then, select your destination.
next Bir sonraki adım Next, confirm your booking.
after that Ondan sonra We had lunch. After that, we visited the museum.
meanwhile Bu sırada · aynı anda I cooked dinner. Meanwhile, my partner set the table.
finally Son olarak · nihayet Finally, we arrived at the hotel at midnight.
İpucu: Adım adım anlatırken First → Then → Next → Finally sırası okuyucuya net bir yol haritası verir. E-posta ve sunumlarda çok etkilidir.
📄

Örnek Paragraf — İş Seyahati (Linking Words Bağlamında)

➕ ekleme ↔️ karşıtlık ⚡ sebep-sonuç 🔢 sıra/zaman
Last month I travelled to London for a business conference. First, I booked my flight online. Then, I reserved a hotel near the venue. The ticket was expensive; however, my company paid for everything. Moreover, they gave me a daily allowance for meals. On the first day, I attended three workshops. Meanwhile, my colleague handled client calls back in Istanbul. Although the schedule was tight, I learned a lot from the sessions. Despite the jet lag, I stayed active throughout the trip. The presentations were clear, and the networking events were very useful. Because I prepared well in advance, I felt confident during my own talk. As a result, I made two new business contacts. Finally, I returned home tired but satisfied.
Geçen ay bir iş konferansı için Londra'ya gittim. Önce uçuşumu online ayırttım. Sonra mekan yakınında otel rezervasyonu yaptım. Bilet pahalıydı; ancak şirketim her şeyi ödedi. Üstelik yemekler için günlük harcırah verdi. İlk gün üç atölyeye katıldım. Bu sırada meslektaşım İstanbul'da müşteri aramalarını yönetti. Program yoğun olmasına rağmen oturumlardan çok şey öğrendim. Jet lag'e rağmen seyahat boyunca aktif kaldım. Sunumlar netti ve networking etkinlikleri çok faydalıydı. Önceden iyi hazırlandığım için kendi konuşmamda kendime güvendim. Sonuç olarak iki yeni iş bağlantısı kurdum. Nihayet yorgun ama memnun eve döndüm.
🇹🇷

Türkçe Konuşanların Klasik Tuzakları

🚨 Although + but birlikte kullanılamaz
Türkçe'de "rağmen ama" diyebilirsin; İngilizce'de although VE but aynı cümlede olmaz — biri yeterli.
✗ Although it was cold, but we went swimming.
✓ Although it was cold, we went swimming.
✓ It was cold, but we went swimming.
🚨 Despite + fiil (yanlış yapı)
Despite / in spite of'tan sonra doğrudan fiil gelmez. İsim, isim tamlaması veya -ing kullan.
✗ Despite he was tired, he worked.
✓ Despite being tired, he worked.
✓ Despite his tiredness, he worked.
✓ Although he was tired, he worked.
So vs Because — kelime sırası
Because sebebi açıklar (sonuç sonra). So sonucu açıklar (sebep önce). Türkçe "-dığı için" her iki yönde de kullanılır — İngilizce'de yön önemli.
✗ So I was tired, I went to bed. (so sonuç için)
✓ I was tired, so I went to bed.
✓ I went to bed because I was tired.
However virgül kuralı
However cümle başında veya ortasında kullanıldığında genellikle virgülle ayrılır. But'tan daha resmidir.
✓ The plan failed. However, we learned from it.
✗ The plan failed however we learned from it.
✓ Although = Though (aynı anlam, farklı ton)
Although daha resmi; though konuşmada daha doğal. Yapı aynı: although/though + clause, main clause.
✓ Though I was busy, I helped her. = Although I was busy, I helped her.
✏️

Önce / Sonra — Paragraf Yeniden Yazımı

Bağlaçsız kısa cümleler → linking words ile akıcı paragraf
✗ Önce — Bağlaçsız
I applied for the job. I updated my CV. I had little experience. I got an interview. The interview went well. The manager liked my attitude. I received an offer. I accepted it. I started last Monday.
Robot gibi duruyor — cümleler birbirinden kopuk.
✓ Sonra — Linking words ile
First, I updated my CV and applied for the job. Although I had little experience, I got an interview. The interview went well, and the manager liked my attitude. As a result, I received an offer, so I accepted it. Finally, I started last Monday.
Akıcı, profesyonel — okuyucu hikâyeyi kolayca takip eder.

Gerçek Hayat Diyaloğu — Seyahat Planı

➕ ekleme ↔️ karşıtlık ⚡ sebep-sonuç 🔢 sıra
A
First, we need to decide on dates. Also, have you checked flight prices?
Önce tarihlere karar vermemiz lazım. Ayrıca uçak fiyatlarına baktın mı?
B
Yes, I checked yesterday. They're quite high because it's peak season.
Evet, dün baktım. Yoğun sezon olduğu için oldukça yüksek.
A
However, if we book early, we might get a discount. Moreover, the hotel has free cancellation.
Ancak erken rezervasyon yaparsak indirim alabiliriz. Üstelik otelin ücretsiz iptali var.
B
Although Rome is expensive, I really want to go. Despite the cost, it's worth it.
Roma pahalı olmasına rağmen gerçekten gitmek istiyorum. Maliyete rağmen buna değer.
A
OK. Then let's book the flights today. After that, we can choose a hotel near the centre.
Tamam. O zaman bugün uçuşları ayıralım. Ondan sonra merkeze yakın bir otel seçeriz.
B
Sounds good. I'll do it now, so we don't miss the cheap seats.
Kulağa iyi geliyor. Ucuz koltukları kaçırmamak için hemen yapayım.

Pratik — Kendini Test Et

A) Doğru bağlacı seç — addition / contrast / cause / sequence
1. I wanted to buy the jacket. , it was too expensive. (karşıtlık)
2. , mix the flour and eggs. , add the milk. (sıra)
3. She didn't come to work she was ill. (sebep)
4. The report is ready. , I've attached the charts. (ekleme · resmi)
5. He passed the exam he studied every day. (sebep)
Cevapları göster
1. However / But   2. First, Then (veya Next)   3. because / since   4. Moreover / In addition / Furthermore   5. because / since
B) Although / Despite / But — doğru yapıyı kur
1. the heavy traffic, we arrived on time.
2. she is new, she learns very quickly.
3. The food was good, the service was slow.
4. having no experience, he got the job.
5. Rewrite: Although it rained, but we stayed outside. →
Cevapları göster
1. Despite / In spite of   2. Although / Though   3. but / however   4. Despite / In spite of   5. Although it rained, we stayed outside. / It rained, but we stayed outside.
C) Hatayı bul ve düzelt
1. Despite he was late, the meeting started. →
2. I was hungry so because I skipped lunch. →
3. Although it was late, but we kept talking. →
4. First we arrived. Finally we checked in. Then we rested. →
Cevapları göster
1. Despite being late / Despite his lateness / Although he was late   2. I was hungry because I skipped lunch. / I skipped lunch, so I was hungry.   3. Although it was late, we kept talking. / It was late, but we kept talking.   4. First we arrived. Then we checked in. Finally we rested.
D) Bağlaçsız cümleleri birleştir
1. The project failed. We learned important lessons. →
2. I love my job. The commute is very long. →
3. Pack your bags. Go to the airport. Board the plane. →
4. Sales dropped. The company cut costs. →
Cevapları göster
1. The project failed; however, we learned important lessons. / Although the project failed, we learned important lessons.   2. I love my job, but the commute is very long. / Although I love my job, the commute is very long.   3. First, pack your bags. Then, go to the airport. Finally, board the plane.   4. Sales dropped. As a result, the company cut costs. / Sales dropped, so the company cut costs.

5 Dakikada Özet

➕Ekleme: and, also, moreover, in addition, furthermore.
↔️Karşıtlık: but, however, although, though, despite/in spite of.
⚡Sebep: because, since. Sonuç: so, therefore, as a result.
🔢Sıra: first → then → next → finally; meanwhile = bu sırada.
🚨Although + but birlikte kullanılmaz. Despite + fiil yanlış.
✍️Linking words paragrafları akıcı ve profesyonel yapar.
